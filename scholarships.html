    Scholarships

    On this page you will learn what scholarships are, the types of scholarships, and what a scholarship entails.

    Why should I apply for scholarships?

    There are many reasons why you should apply for a scholarship.

  • The cost of college/university increases every year
  • Debt is rising and hiring is falling
  • Enjoy colleges without stressing over money
  • You can also apply it to financial aid if you do not receive a full ride

    • Types of Scholarships

    There are many different scholarships around the world but you want to make sure that you choose the right one for you. You can choose from many different scholarships based on your plans for college/university. You can choose from different scholarship categories such as academic, your background, athletic, financial aid eligibility, etc. Academic scholarships can be based off of your test scores, your field of study, or your GPA. Background scholarships may be based off of your culture, religion, or ethnicity. Athletic scholarships are based on the school you choose, because not all schools are D1 or D2.(D3 schools do not offer athletic scholarships)

    Requirements and steps to qualify for a scholarship

    Example of scholarship requirements:

  • Must be a senior in highschool
  • Must have cumulative GPA of a 3.0 or higher
  • Top 10% of their class
  • SAT score of 1300 or higher/ ACT of 28

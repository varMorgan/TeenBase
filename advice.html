Advice

This page is about helping other young students on their pathway towards college and how to remain successful throughout highschool.

Advice Begins

Take advantage of all volunteer opportunities within your community and school. Also when searching for volunteer opportunities try to volunteer at a certain organization for a good amount of time because colleges tend to see how committed you actually are.

Always seek help whenever you are struggling in a certain subject don't hesitate to ask any questions because it is better to know and understand than not understand at all.

Do not procrastinate! (I know you have probably heard this a million times but it is true!!)

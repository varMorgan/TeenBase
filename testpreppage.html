Test Prep and Tips

Unfortunately, test taking becomes more and more prevalent during your highschool years. Everyone approaches test taking differently, but we want to share some of the resources and tips that were most helpful for us.

Good Luck, -Admin MASTER Morgan :)

AP

Crash Course Series: I found this resource very helpful when I took the AP World History exam. The information is quickly introduced and there are visuals. -Admin Morgan

The Princeton Review Books: I have only taken the AP World and AP Human Geo tests, but these books were very helpful. They have practice test and quick reviews over each unit. Also, if you do not want to buy a new one, ask around school and, more often than not, someone else will have on from a previous year. You could also check half priced books. They are just as helpful. -Admin Morgan
